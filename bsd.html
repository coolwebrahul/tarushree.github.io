Blind Spot Detection

By: Tarushree Gandhi, Bhomik Luthra           [The Hi-tech Robotic Systemz Pvt. Ltd.]

Commercial vehicles are big and often suffer from very poor visibility, despite being generously equipped with mirrors. Almost invariably, the driver cannot see important areas beside the vehicle. Blind Spot Detection aims at detecting vehicles in the blind spot of the vehicle in use.
In our implementation, cameras are installed on the back of the truck, over the sides, capturing the entire blind spot. Object detection is then used to detect presence of vehicles in the concerned region of interest. Detection of vehicles in the blind spot help us warn drivers before turning. Hence, it prevents road accidents.
I am currently working towards solving this problem. Model accuracy acheived so far is 68.5% on test dataset.

Algorithm, Framework used : HOG, SVM, Dlib (Programming Language - C++)
