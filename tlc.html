Traffic Light Classification

By: Tarushree Gandhi          [The Hi-tech Robotic Systemz Pvt. Ltd.]

A convolutional neural network has been used to determine the presence and state of traffic lights in road scene images captured from camera mounted on a vehicle. Images are classified into Red light/ Green light/ No light.
I have implemented and modified DenseNet network to achieve a high accuracy of 98.4% on test data, running with a speed of 8 fps on Nvidia TX1.

Network, Framework used : DenseNet, Darknet (programming language - C)
