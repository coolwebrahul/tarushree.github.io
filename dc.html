Dual Classifier

By: Tarushree Gandhi           [The Hi-tech Robotic Systemz Pvt. Ltd.]

As huge amounts of data get accumulated, an inevitable need for sorting through the datasets arises. In case of images, we are often required to segregate images of particular types or images containing specific features.
To address this need, I implemented a convolutional neural network to achieve two objectives simultaneously i.e.
(i) Classifying images into 2 classes (day and night) to find out whether the image was captured during day or night.
(ii) Classifying images as interesting/ non-interesting - In this implementation, images containing traffic lights, tunnels, bridges, flyovers, rain, fog, glare or animals are considered unusual (interesting).
Model accuracy achieved - 92.3% on test dataset.

Network, Framework used : Modified Squeezenet network, Caffe (Programming Language - C++)

**Note : Output for each image has been printed over the image. Each image has been classified as Day/ Night and Int(interesting)/ Unint(Un-interesting))
